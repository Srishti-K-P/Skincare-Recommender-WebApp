Help & FAQs

Getting Started

Welcome to MyGoldenSkin.com! We're here to help you find the perfect skincare products for your unique needs.

  • How do I use MyGoldenSkin.com?

    • Simply answer a few questions about your skin type, concerns, and preferences. Our recommendation engine will then suggest the best products for you.

  • What kind of questions will I be asked?

    • We'll ask you about your skin type (dry, oily, combination, etc.), your main skin concerns (wrinkles, acne, dryness, etc.).

  • Do I have to create an account?

    • No, you don't need to create an account to use MyGoldenSkin.com.

Understanding Recommendations

We take a science-backed approach to product recommendations, considering ingredients, brand reputation, and user reviews.We also take into account your individual skin type and concerns.
